About Us

Our Humble Beginnings

OMTIMUS

I’m Siva Reddy, the founder and director of OMTIMUS Private Limited, which I established in 2017. Under this company, I am thrilled to share and express my lifelong passion for toys, which inspired me to create unique products that combine the enjoyment of play with the benefits of learning. Growing up, toys played a significant role in my development, helping me build various skills and providing endless joy.

Due to the vast and competitive nature of the creative aspects of the toy market, I sought to innovate by integrating craft activities into a creative book format. This approach aims to offer children an engaging experience that fosters skills like creativity and hand-eye coordination while also providing educational value. For parents, these are convenient, affordable, and enriching products that combine play with learning.

Our products stand out by merging the interactive elements of toys with the constructive aspects of crafts, presenting a distinctive option in the toy market. Our aim is to engage children and their families in colorful, creative hand-eye coordination activities that help reduce their dependency on screen time and keep them away from electronic gadgets.



CRAFTS `N` BOOKS

Children are highly impressionable and have a remarkable ability to learn from their surroundings. Understanding this, Mr. Siva Reddy, Founder and Director of Omtimus Private Limited, has introduced a unique brand called “CRAFTS ‘N’ BOOKS.” As a passionate enthusiast of toys and games, He sought to create a product that aligns with his values of sustainability and creativity. Opting for eco-friendly paper and cardboard over plastic, he developed a series of books designed to inspire children through arts and crafts. This approach provides a creative and environmentally-conscious way for children to engage and learn.
