About Us

Our Humble Beginnings

  • 2018-2019

    Crafts In Books

    The children, the tiny little beings are impressionable. They can quickly adapt and learn from the sources available to them. Based on this concept, Mr. Siva Reddy, the Founder and Director of Omtimus Private Limited has launched brand of books, which he named as “Crafts in Books”. The man himself is an ardent lover of toy and games. He has been always interested in making toys, but could not achieve the results until now, as he feels plastic or other materials are way too expensive. So, he chose paper because of its affordability and eco-friendly attribute. He knew that children can explore the creative world better through arts and crafts.
